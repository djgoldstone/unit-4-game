Dubs Challenge

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four players below. By clicking on them you will add a specific amount of point to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each player is hidden from you until clicked on.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each player.
